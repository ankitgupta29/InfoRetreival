SENSIO Signs Licensing Agreement With Paramount Pictures to Bring 3D Movies to 3DGO!(TM)
MONTREAL, QUEBEC--(Marketwire - March 25, 2013) - SENSIO Technologies Inc. (SENSIO) (TSX VENTURE:SIO) today announced a 3D VOD licensing agreement with Paramount Pictures to make select 3D movies available for on-demand rental on SENSIO's 3DGO! service.
